Skincare Basics

Know Your Skin Type

Understanding whether your skin is dry, oily, combination, or sensitive is the first step to an effective routine.

Essential Steps

Every good skincare routine includes cleansing, moisturizing, and sun protection regardless of skin type.

Consistency is Key

Results don't happen overnightâ€”give your products time to work and maintain your routine daily.

Morning Routine

1

Gentle Cleanser

Start with a mild cleanser to remove overnight oil buildup without stripping your skin.

2

Toner

Balance your skin's pH and prepare it to better absorb the following products.

3

Vitamin C Serum

Apply antioxidant protection against environmental damage and brighten your complexion.

4

Moisturizer

Lock in hydration with a lightweight moisturizer appropriate for your skin type.

5

Sunscreen

Finish with SPF 30+ sunscreen, the most important anti-aging product in your routine.

Evening Routine

1

Double Cleanse

Remove makeup and SPF with an oil cleanser, then follow with a water-based cleanser.

2

Exfoliate

Use chemical exfoliants (AHAs/BHAs) 2-3 times weekly to remove dead skin cells.

3

Treatment Serum

Apply targeted treatments like retinol, niacinamide, or peptides to address specific concerns.

4

Eye Cream

Gently pat eye cream around the orbital bone to hydrate and treat this delicate area.

5

Night Moisturizer

Seal everything in with a richer moisturizer to repair your skin barrier overnight.

Recommended Products

For Dry Skin

  • Hydrating cream cleanser
  • Hyaluronic acid serum
  • Rich moisturizer with ceramides
  • Facial oil for nighttime

For Oily Skin

  • Foaming or gel cleanser
  • Salicylic acid treatment
  • Oil-free moisturizer
  • Clay mask twice weekly

For Combination Skin

  • Balancing cleanser
  • Niacinamide serum
  • Lightweight lotion
  • Targeted treatments for T-zone
